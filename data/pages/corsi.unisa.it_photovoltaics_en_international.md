## Photovoltaics International
The PHOTOVOLTAICS PhD program encourages students to stay abroad for at least one semester, also taking advantage of active international agreements.General information is available on the University web page relating to the International section: <https://web.unisa.it/international>
The PHOTOVOLTAICS PhD program also encourages the achievement of international double/multiple degrees. Students can submit their proposals to the Board of Professors by following the rules reported at the link on this page.
