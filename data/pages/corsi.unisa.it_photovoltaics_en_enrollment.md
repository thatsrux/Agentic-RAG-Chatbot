#  Photovoltaics | Enrollment
## Photovoltaics Enrollment
Access to the Italian PhD Program in “Photovoltaics” is through a public competition.
An oral examination is required. The interview involves the presentation and discussion of the project proposed by the candidate and is aimed at assessing the candidate's aptitude for scientific research and their general knowledge on topics relevant to the PhD program. For more details on these topics, please refer to the following webpage:
<https://corsi.unisa.it/photovoltaics/en/home>
The webpage for the admission competition calls for the PhD Program is as follows:
https://web.unisa.it/didattica/dottorati/bandi
Notices, information, and application forms are available in the PhD section of the university website.
