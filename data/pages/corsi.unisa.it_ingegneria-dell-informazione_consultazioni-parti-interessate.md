## Consultazioni Parti Interessate
The PhD Program considers Stackholders Consultations of fundamental importance to maintain an active and constant dialogue with the interested stakeholders, professional societies, PhD alumni, doctoral students in progress and teachers, both in the planning and establishment phase and in the process of reviewing the educational plan.
