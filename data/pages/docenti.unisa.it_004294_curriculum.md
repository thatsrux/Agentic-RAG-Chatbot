#  Giovanni SPAGNUOLO | Curriculum
## Curriculum Docente
**Curriculum Vitae et Studiorum of Giovanni Spagnuolo**
Giovanni Spagnuolo was born in Salerno (Italy) on 12 September 1967.
**Current position and affiliation**
Full Professor of Electrical Engineering at DIEM ([www.diem.unisa.it](http://www.diem.unisa.it)) Department of Information Engineering and Electrical Engineering and Applied Mathematics of the University of Salerno ([www.unisa.it](http://www.unisa.it)).
**Studies**
  * He received the M.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineering from the University of Salerno (Italy) on April 5th, 1993
  * He received the D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University "Federico II" of Naples in 1998.


**Employment at the University of Salerno (Italy)**
  * From November 1999 to December 2003 he was Assistant Professor of Electrical Engineering.
  * From January 1st, 2004 until April 2017 he was Associate Professor of Electrical Engineering.
  * Since May 2016 he is Full Professor of Electrical Engineering.


**Visiting Professor at Tampere University of Technology (Finland)**
  * From August 2018 to October 2018 (three months) he was Visiting Professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering.


**Institutional roles**
  * National Coordinator of the Italian PhD Program (Dottorato di Ricerca di Interesse Nazionale) on “Photovoltaics” (https://corsi.unisa.it/photovoltaics).


**Memberships**
  * From 2010 to 2015 he was Senior Member of the IEEE.
  * Since January 2016 he is Fellow of the IEEE “For contributions to control of photovoltaic systems”.
  * Since 1996 he is member of the “Ordine degli Ingegneri della Provincia di Salerno” (Italian organization of professional engineers)


**Editorial Activities**
  * From January 2011 to January 2022 he served as Member of the Steering Committee and Editor, for the topic “PV system control”, of the IEEE Journal of Photovoltaics
  * Since January 2022 he is Member of the Advisory Board of the IEEE Journal of Photovoltaics
  * From January 2007 to June 2019 he was Associate Editor of the IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics.
  * From April 2008 to December 2012 he was Associate Editor of the International Journal of Industrial Electronics and Drives, Inderscience Publishers Ltd.
  * Co-Guest Editor of two Special Issues of Elsevier journal Mathematics and Computers in Simulation dedicated to the Electrimacs 2014 and Electrimacs 2017 post-conference papers.
  * Since 2010, co-Guest Editor of six Special Sections of the IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics, on photovoltaic systems and fuel cells.
  * From May 2020 to March 2021 Section Editor in Chief of the Section “Solar Energy and Photovoltaic Systems Section” of MDPI Energies journal.
  * Since January 2021 Associate Editor of the IEEE Open Journal of the Industrial Electronics Society.
  * Since January 2023 Associate Editor and also Editor of “Book News” in the IEEE Industrial Electronics Magazine.
