#  Electrical Engineering for Digital Energy | Project meeting - FLORIN "FLuorescent nanO-agents for super-Resolution Imaging and seNsing" - Horizon Europe project (GA 101086142)
## Electrical Engineering for Digital Energy Project meeting - FLORIN "FLuorescent nanO-agents for super-Resolution Imaging and seNsing" - Horizon Europe project (GA 101086142)
Ad un anno dall'inizio delle attività in programma, cogliendo l'occasione della contemporanea presenza di diversi partner alla conferenza internazionale IEEE[ Patrizia Lamberti](https://docenti.unisa.it/005610/home) del [DIEM ](https://www.diem.unisa.it/)e le prof.sse [Silvana Morello ](https://docenti.unisa.it/021670/home)e [Patrizia Gazzerro](https://docenti.unisa.it/005899/home) del [DiFarma ](https://www.difarma.unisa.it/)che stanno prendendo parte attiva nel progetto finanziato come 
Di seguito l'agenda dettagliata dell'evento.
10.30-10.45 Florin results at NMDC2023, by prof. Patrizia Lamberti, PIUNISA and WP3 leader, University of Salerno (ITALY)
10.45-11.00 Multiphysics modelling of cells and nanoparticles: latest results fromUNISA team, by prof. Elisabetta Sieni and prof. Patrizia Lamberti, University of Salerno
11.00-11.15 MB Platformina visit and possible advancement from Pharmacological perspective, by prof. Silvana Morello -UNISA team member and ER secondee at MB Platformina partner (Vilnius, LT).
11.15-11.30 MB Platformina visit and possible advancement from Pathologist perspective, by prof. Patrizia Gazzerro -UNISA team member and ER secondee at MB Platformina partner (Vilnius, LT).
11.30-11.45 Ongoing experimental interactions between FMTC, Platformina and UNISA teams for Florin project, by Dr. Renata Karpicz, FTMC group leader, Vilnius (LITHUANIA)
11.45-12.00 Interaction on nanoparticles modeling for bio-application, by Yaraslau Padrez - FTMC team member and ESR secondee atUNISA partner (University of Salerno, IT).
12.15-12.30 Narrando SME and possible interaction with FLORIN project, by prof. Paolo Ciambelli, CEO of Narrando SRL Salerno (ITALY)
12.30-13.00 Discussion
13.00-13.15 Planning of the following secondments between Platformina, FMTC andUNISA partners, by P. Lamberti and R. Karpicz
13.15-13.30 Conclusion and remarks
23 Ottobre 2023 da [LAMBERTI Patrizia](https://docenti.unisa.it/005610 "LAMBERTI Patrizia")
