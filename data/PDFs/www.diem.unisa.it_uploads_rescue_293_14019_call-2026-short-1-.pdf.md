The 2025-2026 editions of the two awards will be launched soon:
• The "Gustavo Sclocchi" Thesis Award, now in its 33rd edition, focuses on energy topics within the Industrial sector.
• The "Ettore Carcione" Thesis Award, celebrating its 3rd edition, is specifically dedicated to excellence in Geothermal Energy.
The awards are dedicated to PhD and Master's theses in STEM disciplines focused on Energy. The best theses will be recognized 
with the Outstanding award for superior quality and exceptional performance, the Excellent award for high quality and 
performance, in addition to specific Special Mentions.
The Award Ceremony will be held in person at the Politecnico di Milano in May 2026. Winners will have the exclusive 
opportunity to meet and discuss their work directly with the numerous industrial sponsors in attendance.
